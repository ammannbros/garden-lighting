Welcome to our short Project Documentation

Hardware

RelaisArray

The board was designed for low power consumption. The overall functionality is very simple. It offers a I2C interface to be addressed by any standard purpose Microcontroller. The boards heard is the well known MCP23017 IO-Expander(input-output) from Microchip.

So the output registers of the chip can be written via I2C. The output registers are connected to a standard OR-Gate to allow the user to control the relys manually through a simple switch. The two inputs are the Output of the MCP23017 registers and the debounced switch. The output of the OR-Gate controles a TTL-Logic-Level MOSFET which switches the indudctors of the rails. (An EAGLE schematic is available for more details)

Issues

A major Issue was, that the MCP2017 resetted after switching the relias if consumers were connected. The relais induced noise to the 12V power line. This noise caused a voltage drop on the reset line of the MCP2017. So after switching the configuration of the Chip got lost. A capacitor from the reset-pin to ground solved the problem.

Improvements

If the load which is connected to the relais is known, a Snubber could be added to improve the durabiltiy of the relays.
